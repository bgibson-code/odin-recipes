Penne A La Vodka

One of my all time favorites, Penne A La Vodka, few meals can match my love for this classic Italian dish.

Packed with flavor, and always an honor to consume, it is no surprise that Penne A La Vodka is a favorite of many. Why not make this masterpiece at home?

With a few simple ingredients we can create this dish in all of its glory to be enjoyed from the comfort of your own home. I usually like to make recipes like this one in bulk to be portioned off and frozen for delectible meal prep.

In the beginning of my cooking journey meals like this one were always intimidating, follow along and see just how easy it is.

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Basil
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Heat Large pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Heat Large pot filled with water to a boil. Add teaspoon of salt to hasten the process.
  3. Wait until water droplets vaporize, then you are heated to perfection
  4. Pour 3-4 tablespoons of olive oil to the pan
  5. Add 3 cloves of minced garlic to the oil
  6. Is that pot of water boiling? Add the Pasta!
  7. Mix garlic around in the oil, not letting it burn, but slightly brown
  8. Once brown, Add 3 tablespoons of dried basil, and 1 qt can of crushed tomatoes
  9. Reduce heat, Mix the contents of the pan regularly, letting it cook down to a sauce.
  10. Check on pasta, remove pasta pot from heat when al-dente. Strain, and set aside
  11. Add 1qt of heavy cream to sauce pan. Mix, and let it incorporate into the sauce
  12. Add cooked pasta into the sauce, mix and let the pasta soak up all of that goodness
  13. Remove from heat, set aside, and Serve! Add a sprinkle of basil and parmasean cheese as desired.
