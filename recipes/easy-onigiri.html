Onigiri

Onigiri, A tasty Japanese classic that no one can turn down.

A whole bunch of tasty Japanese goodness wrapped up and portable, whats not to like. Why not make Onigiri at Home?

With a few simple ingredients we can create this dish in all of its glory to be enjoyed from the comfort of your own home. I usually like to make recipes like this one in bulk to be portioned off and frozen for delectible meal prep.

In the beginning of my cooking journey meals like this one were always intimidating, follow along and see just how easy it is.

Ingredients

  • Short Grain White Rice
  • Meat of your choice, fish, chicken, whatever you like
  • Sesame seeds if thats your thing
  • Nori
  • Japanese mayo
  • Sauces, sriracha, soy sauce, take your pic
  • Parchment paper, or plastic wrap

Steps

  1. Cook the rice, in a rice cooker, instant-pot, whatever, you can figure that out
  2. Cook/prepare your meat of choice
  3. Add Sauces and spices as desired
  4. lay out your parchment paper, and flatten a layer of rice onto it, make a 4 inch diameter blob of rice
  5. Add your meat of choice to the center, about a spoon full
  6. Mold the rice around the meat using the parchment, mold it into a triangular shape
  7. Cut a strip of nori, wrap around the bottom of your triangle like a little handle.
  8. EAT IT
